Register for a government ID account

You can choose to register with Government Gateway or GOV.UK Verify. Either account type will let you prove your identity and check your State Pension.

It usually takes 15 minutes to register.

Government Gateway

Registering with Government Gateway usually takes about 10 minutes. It works best if you have your National Insurance number, plus either:

  • a P60 or recent payslip
  • a valid UK passport
Create a Government Gateway account

GOV.UK Verify

Registering with GOV.UK Verify works best if you have a UK address, plus either:

  • a valid passport
  • a photocard driving licence
Create a GOV.UK Verify account

A certified company will double check your identity when you register with GOV.UK Verify. They’ve all met security standards set by government.

Why you need to register

Government Gateway and GOV.UK Verify are secure services built to fight identity theft. Once you’ve registered, you can reuse your account to sign in to other government services online.
